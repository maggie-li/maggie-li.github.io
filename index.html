Volunteer Experience

Welcoming Newcomers and Refugees to Canada - July 2017

This was a 3-day volunteer experience. On the first day we were trained to properly greet the families, and direct them to the proper resources to help them get started on their new life here in Canada. We also spent the first day making tee shirts to wear, setting up games for the children to play, and decorating the floor to create a welcoming atmosphere. On the second day we welcomed the families, I was glad that I was able to communicate and translate for many families.

This experience was especially touching for me as my parents are immigrants. I sympathize with the parents who choose to embark on this journey in a new country to provide their children with a better life than they had. Moving to a new country is extremely difficult, all I could’ve hoped to do was make their journey easier on them in some way.

Snowboarding

Once you stop falling, you then begin to enjoy it. If you stay, you will never see why people love it so much.” - William Nepe
